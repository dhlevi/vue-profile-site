About Me

I am a senior software developer/consultant/architect in Victoria, British Columbia, Canada, with over 15 years of experience working with government and private sector clients. I am a full-stack developer with projects covering UI and front end through to the design and development of back-end API's and services utilizing a number of different languages, frameworks, and technologies from Oracle reports to CouchDB to FME, various programming languages, and more tools than I can think of at one sitting.. I have lead teams in large scale projects using both agile and traditional methodologies. I have specialized in spatial technologies and developing GIS solutions throughout my career, from web mapping applications to server side processing of large scale spatial data.

I love all aspects of designing and developing good software that solves a clients real needs. As a "people person", I enjoy working directly with my clients to really get to know them, their business, and understand how software solutions can improve their workflows.
